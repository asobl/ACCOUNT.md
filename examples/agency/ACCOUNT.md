## HEAD

Basecamp Creative is a 6-person brand strategy agency in Denver, CO. They specialize in positioning and launch campaigns for consumer packaged goods (CPG) startups. Three team members use the product: Jordan (founder), Suki (strategist), and one rotating junior.

**Why they use us:** "We were keeping client notes in Google Docs and it was chaos. Nobody could find anything before a call. I needed one file per client that everyone could read in 2 minutes." (Jordan Bell, September 2025 signup)

**How they found us:** Jordan found the "account context for small teams" blog post via organic search. Signed up same day. No trial period, went straight to starter plan.

**Configuration:** Starter plan, 3 seats. No integrations. Jordan manually updates files after every client call.
